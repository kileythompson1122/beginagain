What is a Trezor Hardware Wallet?

A Trezor hardware wallet is a physical device that securely stores your cryptocurrency offline. Unlike software wallets, which are connected to the internet and prone to hacking, Trezor wallets keep your private keys completely offline, making them immune to cyberattacks.
